A student-run global astronomy initiative providing free space science curricula, coding-based projects, and research opportunities to students worldwide.

## Mission

AstroReach makes astronomy education accessible to all students, regardless of resources or location. We provide comprehensive curricula, Python-based projects, and collaborative research opportunitiesâ€”completely free.

## What We Offer

- **Comprehensive Astronomy Curricula** - Structured lessons from basic concepts to advanced astrophysics
- **Coding Projects** - Python simulations, NASA data analysis, orbital mechanics
- **Research Opportunities** - Exoplanet analysis, light pollution mapping, citizen science
- **Club Support** - Everything needed to start and run astronomy clubs
- **Global Network** - Connect with students worldwide

## Featured Projects

- Light Pollution Mapping
- Exoplanet Data Analysis (NASA Kepler/TESS)
- ISS Tracking & Orbital Mechanics
- Galaxy Classification
- Solar Observation Protocols
- N-body Simulations

## Impact

- 50+ schools reached across 15+ countries
- 500+ students engaged
- 100% free and open access
